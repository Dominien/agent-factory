# Validation Scenarios

## Scenario 1: Well-known national chain
**Input**: "Check Bright Horizons in San Jose, CA"
**Expected**: Score 7-10, TRUSTED. National chain, licensed, NAEYC-accredited, many reviews.

## Scenario 2: Small local daycare
**Input**: "Research Little Stars Academy in Austin, Texas"
**Expected**: Score varies. May find limited online presence for a smaller operation.

## Scenario 3: Red flag indicators
**Input**: "A home daycare called 'Happy Kids' wants me to pay $2,000 upfront before I can even visit. They say they're licensed but won't share the license number. Located in Miami, FL."
**Expected**: Score 1-4, HIGH RISK. Won't share license, large upfront payment, no visit allowed.
